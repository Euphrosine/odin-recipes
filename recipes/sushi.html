Description

A step-by-step guide to making delicious sushi rolls filled with fresh ingredients and flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 4 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Nori sheets
  • Sushi fillings (e.g., fresh fish, avocado, cucumber, crab sticks)
  • Soy sauce and wasabi for serving

Steps

  1. Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Place the rice in a saucepan with water and cook according to package instructions.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved.
  4. Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large bowl and pour the vinegar mixture over it.
  5. Using a wooden spatula, gently fold and mix the rice to evenly distribute the vinegar seasoning.
  6. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat or a clean kitchen towel.
  7. Spread a thin layer of seasoned sushi rice on the nori, leaving a small border at the top.
  8. Add your desired fillings in a line across the center of the rice.
  9. Roll up the sushi tightly using the mat or towel, applying gentle pressure to shape it.
  10. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  11. Slice the sushi rolls into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife.
  12. Serve the sushi with soy sauce and wasabi.
