Description

A classic homemade pizza recipe that will delight your taste buds with its delicious flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions)

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add the warm water and olive oil, then mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Roll out the dough into a pizza shape and transfer it to a baking sheet.
  7. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border around the edges.
  8. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  9. Add your desired toppings.
  10. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
